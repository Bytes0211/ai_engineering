


# ASRV Company Brochure

---

## About ASRV

ASRV is a premium sportswear and activewear brand specializing in high-performance gym clothes designed for those who live and breathe fitness. They offer a sophisticated range of mens and womens apparel and accessories crafted with cutting-edge technology to support running, training, and recreational activities.

---

## Product Range

ASRV’s collections include:

- **Men’s & Women’s Apparel:** Tops, tanks, sports bras, hoodies, jackets, shorts, joggers, leggings, pants.
- **Accessories:** Hats, headwear, bags, chest packs, gloves, sleeves, socks, underwear, jewelry, and hardware.
- **Seasonal Collections:** Winter, Fall, Summer, along with special capsules like Tech Essential™ and Graphics Capsule.
- **Collaborations:** ASRV x New Era®.

All products leverage innovative fabric technologies such as Tetra-Lite®, AeroSilver®, Supima® Cotton, Laser-Vent™, Aerotex™, Tech Essential™, and Lycra® for superior comfort, breathability, and durability.

---

## Company Philosophy & Culture

At ASRV, the ethos revolves around the fusion of design, technology, and mindset — crafting not just activewear but mindset-enhancing apparel. Their philosophy embraces mindfulness, mental toughness, and the pursuit of peak performance both physically and mentally.

ASRV promotes a holistic "Mindset Training" culture encouraging mental resilience through newsletters, music for the mind, and storytelling to inspire athletes and customers alike.

---

## Design Principles

ASRV’s design approach is rooted in meticulous craftsmanship with attention to detail, ensuring each piece offers functionality without compromising style. Their clothing is engineered to perform during intense physical activities, incorporating fabric innovations that enhance cooling, comfort, and mobility.

## Customers & Market

ASRV caters to fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and anyone dedicated to an active lifestyle who demand premium quality, technology-driven sportswear. Their offerings are well-suited for running, training, and recreational use, appealing to a broad spectrum of consumers looking for both performance and aesthetic excellence.
